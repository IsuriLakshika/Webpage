How Our Cosmic Neighborhood Came to Be

The Solar System, our cosmic home, was born about 4.6 billion years ago from the collapse of a massive molecular cloud of gas and dust. Through a fascinating process driven by gravity and cosmic forces, the Sun, planets, moons, asteroids, and comets came into existence.

This celestial birth provided the conditions for life to emerge billions of years later on Earth.

  • Age: ~4.6 billion years
  • Central Object: The Sun
  • Planetary Count: 8 planets + dwarf planets
  • Formation Process: Gravitational collapse of a solar nebula
